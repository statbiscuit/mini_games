Design-o-gram

How to Play Design-o-gram

Your goal is to recreate the target grid by toggling cells to match the patterns indicated by the row and column clues.

  • Click a cell to toggle it on/off.
  • The game is won when all rows and columns match their respective clues.

Understanding the Clues

The numbers outside the grid represent groups of consecutive filled cells:

  • A **comma-separated hint** (e.g., 3, 2) means there is at least one empty cell between the groups.
  • A **whitespace-separated hint** (e.g., 1 2) also means groups are separated by at least one empty cell.
  • A single number (e.g., 5) represents one continuous group of filled cells.

Hints and Tips

Here are some tips to help you solve the puzzle:

  • Start with rows or columns that have the largest numbers.
  • Use the numbers to infer which cells must remain empty.
  • Revisit the guide anytime by clicking the "Help" button.
